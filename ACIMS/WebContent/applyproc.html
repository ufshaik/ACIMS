  How to apply for Assessment and Accreditation
Annexure-1 || Annexure-2
 

The institution should submit a Letter of Intention in the format (Annexure-1), along with the Information of the Institution (Annexure-II), for assessment and accreditation. NAAC scrutinises the information to determine the eligibility and sends the manuals and guidelines that enable the institution to initiate the process of self-assessment.
