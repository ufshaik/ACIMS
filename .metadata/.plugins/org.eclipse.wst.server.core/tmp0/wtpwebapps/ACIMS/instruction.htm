Instructions for Students

Please follow below instructions to fill the Application Form

·        Each candidate is required to take an individual DD for the registration fee and the candidate is required to mention Name, Hall ticket No, College on the back side of the DD with out fail

·        All fields are mandatory

·        Use Block / Capital Letters to fill the application form

·        Provide the University Hall Ticket Number in the Field#1

·        Provide Religion as Christian / Muslim / Hindu / Sikh / Jain / Buddha and etc

·        Provide your EAMCET rank with which you have joined in engineering

·        Provide Working E-Mail ID & Alternative E-Mail ID, as these are used to send all the information like whether you are eligible for any company, to which center you got allotted and when the companies are scheduled. Please ensure that the mailed who you are mentioning is in working condition.

·        Candidates are required to attend the trainings organized near by JKC College or in their college

·        Candidates have to attend the Recruitment Processes organized regionally in JKC colleges

·        Candidates who are not meeting the eligibility criteria are strictly advised to not send the application forms.

·        Candidates should submit their application form along with DD to college principal / placement officer

·        Individual applications from the candidates will not be accepted

·        Once the application along with the DD is received at IEG, registration amount will not be refunded in any condition

Post Selection by any company:

IEG is offering training on 10-modules and JKC training on projects to gain the confidence of the industry.

Foundational IT Skills Training

All the selected students by any company must take training on 10 foundational modules offered by IEG. This training will be arranged in holidays.

The training fee Rs.10, 000/- should be sent to IEG with in 10 days post-selection in the form of DD drawn in the favor of “Institute for Electronic Governance”.

JKC Training

All the selected students by any company have to undergo JKC Training Program* at JKC Hosting Institutions.

Students from SC/ST Category with white ration card

  • Registration Fee: Rs. 500/-
  • All other fee is exempted.

Students from SC/ST Category without white ration card

  • Registration fee: Rs. 500/-
  • Fee for 10-Modules post selection: Rs. 10,000/- ; JKC Training Fee: Rs. 8,000/-

To avail this students are required to attach the attested caste certificate and / or white ration card along with the CPM Application Form
